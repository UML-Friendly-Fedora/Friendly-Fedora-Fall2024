It's a test suite that makes sure a Consumer class behaves as needed inside the compose_tracker module to process Fedora Messaging messages related to Fedora Compose system, specifically: handling of logs, maintainers, and several statuses of the compose process.This suite uses the pytest framework and pytest-mock to simulate real-world situations and ensure that the Consumer will behave accordingly in any given circumstance. Furthermore, in-depth testing is performed with a wide variety of inputs in addition to the various edge cases to ensure robustness and reliability of the system.
